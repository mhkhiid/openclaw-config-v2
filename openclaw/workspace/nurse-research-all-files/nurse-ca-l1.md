## 1. 加拿大移民局官方政策（IRCC）

### 1.1 Federal Skilled Worker Program (FSWP) 联邦技术工人项目

#### 官方链接
- **IRCC官网**：https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/who-can-apply/federal-skilled-workers.html

#### 原文摘录
> "In order to be eligible to apply under the Federal Skilled Worker Program, potential candidates must meet the minimum requirements for work, language ability, education and obtain at least 67 points under the program's 100-point grid."
> 
> **最低要求**：
> - One year of continuous full-time or equivalent paid work experience in the past 10 years in a skilled occupation classified under the NOC TEER level 0, 1, 2, or 3
> - Validated language ability equivalent to Canadian Language Benchmark (CLB) 7 in English or French across all abilities (reading, writing, listening, and speaking)
> - Canadian educational credential (certificate, diploma, or degree) or foreign credential and Educational Credential Assessment (ECA) report
> - Get at least 67 out of 100 points on the FSWP grid

#### 67分评分标准

| 评分因素 | 最高分数 |
|---------|---------|
| 教育 (Education) | 25分 |
| 语言能力 (Language Skills) | 28分 |
| 工作经验 (Work Experience) | 15分 |
| 年龄 (Age) | 12分 |
| 预先安排就业 (Arranged Employment) | 10分 |
| 适应能力 (Adaptability) | 10分 |
| **总分** | **100分** |

#### 中文解读
- **护士适用性**：护士属于NOC 31301（Registered nurses and registered psychiatric nurses），TEER Level 1，符合FSWP职业要求
- **核心门槛**：需要67分以上才能入池，但入池后还需CRS高分才能获邀（2024年平均CRS分数470-480分）
- **处理时间**：Express Entry处理时间约6个月
- **资金证明**：需要提供定居资金证明（2024年单身约$13,757 CAD，四口之家约$25,564 CAD）

### 1.2 Canadian Experience Class (CEC) 加拿大经验类

#### 官方链接
- **IRCC官网**：https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/who-can-apply/canadian-experience-class.html

#### 原文摘录
> "Under the Canadian Experience Class, you must:
> - have at least one year of cumulative paid full-time work experience (or the equivalent in paid part-time work) in Canada in TEER Category 0, 1, 2 or 3 of the NOC
> - Your work experience must have been obtained within the last three years from the date of submitting your application
> - Work experience must be gained in Canada while maintaining legal status"

#### CEC vs FSWP 工作经验对比

| 项目 | CEC | FSWP |
|-----|-----|------|
| 工作地点要求 | 必须在加拿大境内 | 可以在任何国家 |
| 工作经验时间 | 过去3年内 | 过去10年内 |
| 连续性要求 | Cumulative（累计） | Continuous（连续） |
| 全职定义 | 每周30小时，共1,560小时 | 每周30小时，共1,560小时 |
| 合法性要求 | 必须持合法工签 | 无特殊要求 |

#### 中文解读
- **护士最佳路径**：对于已有加拿大工签并工作的护士，CEC是首选路径
- **分数优势**：CEC申请人通常已有加拿大工作经验，CRS得分较高
- **注意**：读书期间的Co-op工作经验**不计入**CEC工作经验

### 1.3 Provincial Nominee Program (PNP) 省提名计划

#### 官方链接
- **IRCC官网**：https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/provincial-nominees.html

#### 1.3.1 Ontario Immigrant Nominee Program (OINP) 安大略省

##### 官方链接
- **安省雇主担保**：https://www.ontario.ca/page/oinp-employer-job-offer-foreign-worker-stream
- **安省EE优才类别**：https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontarios-express-entry-human-capital-priorities-stream

##### 原文摘录（雇主担保类别）
> "The job offer must be for a full-time and permanent position. Full-time means the job must be a minimum of 1,560 hours a year and a minimum of 30 hours of paid work per week."
> 
> "The job offered must be in a skilled occupation at TEER category 0, 1, 2 or 3 of the National Occupational Classification (NOC)."
> 
> "You must have at least two years of cumulative paid full-time work experience (or the equivalent in paid part-time work) in the same occupation (same NOC code) as your job offer."

##### 安省雇主担保评分因素

| 评分因素 | 最高分 | 说明 |
|---------|-------|------|
| **NOC TEER类别** | 10分 | TEER 0/1=10分，TEER 2/3=8分 |
| **职业大类** | 10分 | 医护类(Occupational Category 0,2,3)=10分 |
| **工资水平** | 10分 | $40+/小时=10分，$20以下=0分 |
| **工签状态** | 10分 | 有有效工签=10分 |
| **在职时间** | 3分 | 6个月以上=3分 |
| **语言能力** | 10分 | CLB 9+=10分，CLB 7=4分 |
| **双语能力** | 10分 | 英法双语=10分 |
| **工作地点** | 10分 | 北安省=10分，GTA以外=8分，多伦多=0分 |

##### 护士特殊豁免（安省EE优才类别）
> "Nurses are not required to demonstrate that they meet this education requirement if they meet both the following conditions:
> - they are registered by the College of Nurses of Ontario (CNO) in the General, Extended, or Temporary class
> - their primary NOC falls under NOC 31300, 31301, 31302, or 32101"

**中文解读**：已在CNO注册的护士申请安省EE优才类别时，**无需提供学历认证(ECA)**。

## 2. 护士注册官方政策

### 2.1 NNAS (National Nursing Assessment Service) 国家护理评估服务

#### 官方链接
- **NNAS官网**：https://www.nnas.ca/
- **资格要求**：https://www.nnas.ca/eligibility/
- **费用说明**：https://www.nnas.ca/fees/

#### 原文摘录（申请资格）
> "You are eligible to apply to NNAS if you have:
> - Completed post-secondary (college or university) nursing education outside of Canada
> - Never been licensed as a nurse in Canada in the profession to which you are applying
> - Have been licensed as a nurse outside of Canada (Expedited Service only)"

#### 原文摘录（两种服务模式）

**Expedited Service（加急服务）**：
> "$750 CAD for multiple professions and regulators. Our Expedited Service provides a simple, fast, and safe way to review your credentials... Advisory Reports are released within five business days of all documents being received."

**Regular Service（常规服务）**：
> "$650 USD (approximately $845 CDN) for one profession and one regulator... Advisory Reports are issued in twelve weeks (about three months)."

#### NNAS费用详表

| 服务类型 | 加急服务 | 常规服务 |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 主申请费 | $750 CAD | $650 USD (~$845 CAD) |
| 同职业额外省份 | 免费 | $55 USD |
| 不同职业额外省份 | 免费 | $220 USD |
| 申请重新激活 | $240 CAD | $180 USD |
| 文件翻译（每页） | $115 CAD | $85 USD |
| 申诉费用 | $500 CAD | $500 USD |

#### 中文解读
- **NNAS作用**：是所有国际护士申请加拿大护士注册的第一步，负责评估海外护理学历
- **评估报告**：完成后会生成Advisory Report，需寄送至目标省份的护理监管机构
- **有效期**：Advisory Report有效期为12个月
- **三种护理职业**：
  - RN (Registered Nurse) - 注册护士
  - LPN (Licensed Practical Nurse) - 执业护士
  - RPN (Registered Psychiatric Nurse) - 注册精神科护士（仅部分省份）

### 2.3 NCLEX-RN考试要求

#### 官方链接
- **Pearson VUE考试中心**：https://www.pearsonvue.com/
- **NCSBN（美国全国护理委员会）**：https://www.ncsbn.org/

#### 考试基本信息

| 项目 | 详情 |
|-----|------|
| 考试名称 | NCLEX-RN (National Council Licensure Examination for Registered Nurses) |
| 考试形式 | 计算机自适应测试 (CAT) |
| 题量 | 75-145题 |
| 时长 | 最长5小时 |
| 合格标准 | 能力达到入门级护士标准 |
| 考试费用 | 约$200 USD + 各省注册费 |
| 重考限制 | 需等待45天 |

#### 中文解读
- **考试内容**：基于临床判断模型，测试安全有效的护理实践能力
- **备考资源**：官方提供NCSBN学习资料和模拟题
- **加拿大适用**：通过NCLEX-RN后，成绩可用于多个省份的注册申请

## 3. 语言要求与学历认证

### 3.1 英语语言要求

#### 3.1.1 雅思 (IELTS)

##### 官方链接
- **IRCC语言要求**：https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/documents/language-requirements.html

##### FSWP语言要求
- **最低要求**：CLB 7（相当于雅思G类：听力6.0，阅读6.0，写作6.0，口语6.0）
- **更高分数优势**：CLB 9（雅思8/7/7/7）可获得更高CRS分数

##### 安省OINP语言要求
> "You must be able to understand, read, write and speak either English or French at a Canadian Language Benchmark (CLB) level 7 or higher."

> "For English tests, we accept:
> - International English Language Testing (IELTS) — we only accept the General Training test. Please note that we do not accept the IELTS One Skill Retake test.
> - Canadian English Language Proficiency Index Program (CELPIP) — we only accept the General test
> - Pearson Test of English (PTE) Core – please note we only accept the PTE Core test, not the PTE Academic test"

#### 3.1.2 CELPIP

##### CLB与雅思/CELPIP对照表

| CLB等级 | 雅思G类（听说读写） | CELPIP（听说读写） |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------|
| 10 | 8.0 / 7.5 / 7.0 / 7.0 | 10 / 10 / 10 / 10 |
| 9 | 8.0 / 7.0 / 7.0 / 7.0 | 9 / 9 / 9 / 9 |
| 8 | 7.5 / 6.5 / 6.5 / 6.5 | 8 / 8 / 8 / 8 |
| 7 | 6.0 / 6.0 / 6.0 / 6.0 | 7 / 7 / 7 / 7 |

### 3.3 中国学历认证

#### 3.3.1 ECA (Educational Credential Assessment) 学历认证

##### 官方链接
- **IRCC指定机构**：https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/documents/education-assessed.html

##### IRCC指定ECA机构

| 机构名称 | 官网 | 特点 |
|---------|------|------|
| **WES** (World Education Services) | https://www.wes.org/ | 最常用，处理时间较快 |
| **ICES** (International Credential Evaluation Service) | https://www.bcit.ca/ices/ | 加拿大BC省机构 |
| **ICAS** (International Credential Assessment Service) | https://www.icascanada.ca/ | 加拿大安省机构 |
| **IQAS** (International Qualifications Assessment Service) | https://www.alberta.ca/iqas.aspx | 阿尔伯塔省政府机构 |
| **CES** (Comparative Education Service - UofT) | https://learn.utoronto.ca/ces | 多伦多大学 |

##### 原文摘录（安省OINP要求）
> "If you completed your studies outside of Canada, you need to obtain an Educational Credential Assessment (ECA) report that proves your education is equivalent to a Canadian credential. The assessment must be done by one of the following organizations, designated by IRCC."
> 
> "The report cannot be more than five years old at the time you submit your application."

#### 3.3.2 护士学历特殊要求

##### NNAS学历评估
- 需要毕业院校直接向NNAS发送成绩单和课程描述
- 需提供护理实践经历证明
- 部分情况需要补充护理课程

#### 中文解读
- **ECA vs NNAS**：ECA用于移民申请，NNAS用于护士注册申请，两者**不能互相替代**
- **时间安排**：建议尽早启动ECA和NNAS申请，处理时间可能需要2-3个月
- **文件准备**：中国学历需准备：毕业证书、学位证书、成绩单（中英文/公证件）

## 6. 关键提示

### 给护士申请人的建议

1. **尽早启动NNAS评估**：这是所有省护士注册的第一步，处理时间较长
2. **同步准备语言考试**：CLB 7是最低门槛，CLB 9可获得更多分数优势
3. **考虑双轨申请**：同时准备Express Entry和PNP申请
4. **关注省提名抽签**：安省、BC省常有针对医护职业的专项邀请
5. **保持工签合法性**：如在加拿大境内，确保护照/工签不过期

### 常见误区

- ❌ NNAS和ECA可以互相替代 → ✅ **必须分别申请**
- ❌ 雅思成绩永久有效 → ✅ **有效期2年**
- ❌ 通过NCLEX后自动获得注册 → ✅ **还需满足各省其他要求**
- ❌ 魁北克只用会英语 → ✅ **法语是魁省移民的核心要求**
